CLASSTALKIES

TO LEARN. TO SHARE. TO GROW.

In today's fast moving world of technology, we students too need a platform to communicate, share our views and help out each other, which is safe, approved by parents and interactive at the same time.
Class Talkies is one such student friendly forum; It is By the students, For the Students and Of the students. Here at Class Talkies, our motto is TO LEARN. TO SHARE. TO GROW.
We aim to create a vast uninterrupted network, where students of all ages can share information pertaining to academics, current affairs, and general awareness, within the best internet safety and confidentiality parameters.
This not only helps the student excel in academics, but to become a conscious and well aware person. We want all students over the world to have access to quality educational material, so that knowledge spreads its light everywhere, and a new generation of able and well educated students is equipped to better the world.
Class Talkies is thus an innovative student initiative, aimed at bringing about a revolutionary change in student communication, keeping up the spirit of collaborative teamwork.
